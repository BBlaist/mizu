Mizu Companion

Welcome to our website!

"Mizu Companion" is a playful water tracking app that makes staying hydrated feel more personal and rewarding. Your digital companion grows happier and more energetic as you log your daily water intake, giving you a small sense of progress with every sip. Designed with a cozy, minimal aesthetic, Mizu turns hydration into a calm, mindful routine — something you look forward to instead of something you have to do.

We’re a small team of designers and developers who wanted to make staying hydrated feel more personal and enjoyable. With "Mizu Companion", we combined our love for nostalgic virtual companions with simple wellness design to create something that feels playful but genuinely useful. Our goal is to turn everyday routines into small, mindful moments. Without the pressure, just a bit of charm and care.
